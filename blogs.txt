## Blog 1: Preventing and Recovering from Stroke in Indian Households

Strokes, or "brain attacks," occur when blood flow to the brain is blocked or bursts, causing sudden weakness, speech slurring, or vision loss—India sees over 1.5 million cases yearly, often from uncontrolled hypertension, diabetes, and high cholesterol fueled by salty pickles, fried snacks, and sedentary jobs. Incorporate heart-protective foods like bajra khichdi, amla juice, and omega-3 from flaxseeds or affordable sardines—studies show turmeric's curcumin cuts clot risk by 25%.
